WELCOME TO SETTLE!

With SETTLE, complex government documents become clear and accessible.
Our AI translates official texts into plain language and provides easy-to-understand explanations. This helps you save time, gain clarity, and make well-informed decisions. We are committed to reducing barriers and fostering transparency in communication with public authorities.

WHY SETTLE?

SETTLE helps you to understand official documents better.
With our combination of translation and AI explanations, we make complicated texts simpler, clearer and accessible to everyone. This allows you to find what is important more quickly – and concentrate on the essentials. Our goal: fewer hurdles, more clarity in everyday dealings with authorities.
